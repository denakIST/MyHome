My name is Deniz Akar. I’m a marketing analytics leader with over 15 years of experience.

Recently I have joined MIT’s FullStack Professional Certificate program to pursue my interest in web development. Throughout my experience I have collaborated with software developers on several projects, and always admired them for having the skills that enable them to take a concept and turn it into a product that people can use. And that has been a catalyst for me to take the leap to join the program.

I have extensive experience in marketing analytics. I started my professional life in predictive statistical modeling which was largely regarded as a 'black box' by the marketers. Fast forward 10-15 years, the proliferation of "Big Data" and "Machine Learning" methods gave black-box a new meaning. This left both providers and clients of data and information with a whole set of new challenges to overcome.

As someone who appreciates the power of taking highly complex and technical topics and communicating them in simple terms, my goal in pursuing fullstack development is to be able to build products that help make analytics the most important part of every professional’s toolbox. This web site is aimed at tracking the journey with the intent of continuously adding, updating and improving it along the way.
